Comparing AI Hiring Demand Across Advanced Economies

Visualization by Kuhika Shrivastava

Created for Visualization Technologies 1: Fundamentals, Professor Steven Geofrey | Northeastern University, Spring 2026

This visualization examines how the share of AI job postings (as a percentage of total job postings) has evolved over time across two analytically defined country groups. Use the buttons to compare established AI economies with smaller advanced economies and observe differences in growth trajectories.
